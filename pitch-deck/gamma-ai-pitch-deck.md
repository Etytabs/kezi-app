# SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM

## The Critical Gap in Women's Health Access

Rwanda faces a significant challenge in women's reproductive health access. Despite progress in healthcare, millions of women still lack the tools, information, and products they need to manage their health with dignity and privacy.

### Problem 1: Limited Contraceptive Access
Only 48% of Rwandan women have access to modern contraceptives. In rural areas, this number drops even lower due to limited health facilities, supply chain challenges, and geographic barriers. Women must often travel long distances to access basic reproductive health products and services.

### Problem 2: Fragmented Health Information
Women lack a centralized, culturally-sensitive platform for cycle tracking, fertility awareness, and maternal health education. Existing apps are designed for Western markets, don't support local languages like Kinyarwanda, and fail to address the unique needs of Rwandan women.

### Problem 3: Stigma and Privacy Concerns
Cultural barriers prevent open discussions about reproductive health. Many women feel uncomfortable seeking information or purchasing products in person. They need discreet, private digital tools they can trust—tools that respect their privacy and cultural context.

### The Result
Women are left without the resources to make informed decisions about their reproductive health, leading to unplanned pregnancies, health complications, and missed opportunities for family planning.

# SLIDE 4: OUR SOLUTION

## Introducing Kezi: Your Complete Women's Health Companion

Kezi is an all-in-one mobile platform that combines intelligent cycle tracking, AI-powered health guidance, and a curated marketplace for women's health products—all designed specifically for Rwandan women.

### Core Feature 1: Intelligent Cycle Tracking
Accurate, science-based cycle predictions with fertility awareness features. Our algorithm considers individual cycle patterns to provide personalized predictions. Multi-profile support allows women at different life stages—from teenagers to mothers—to track their unique health journeys.

### Core Feature 2: AI Health Companion
Powered by advanced AI (GPT-4), Kezi provides personalized health guidance based on your current cycle phase. Available in three languages—English, French, and Kinyarwanda—the AI understands cultural context and provides relevant, respectful advice. It remembers your preferences and health history for increasingly personalized support.

### Core Feature 3: Curated Marketplace
A carefully selected collection of women's health products from verified Rwandan merchants. Products are intelligently recommended based on your cycle phase—menstrual care products during your period, wellness supplements during follicular phase, fertility products during ovulation. All payments supported through MTN MoMo and Airtel Money.

### Core Feature 4: Privacy First Design
We understand that privacy is paramount. Kezi includes biometric authentication (fingerprint/face), Discreet Mode to hide sensitive information, and Privacy Veil to protect your screen from prying eyes. Your data stays private and secure.

### Core Feature 5: Wellness Journal
Track symptoms, moods, energy levels, and health patterns over time. Build a comprehensive picture of your health with historical insights that help you understand your body better and have more informed conversations with healthcare providers.

### Core Feature 6: Rwanda-First Design
Built specifically for the Rwandan market with local geolocation, RWF currency throughout, Mobile Money payments (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money), and full support for Kinyarwanda language. This is not a Western app translated—it's designed for Rwandan women from the ground up.

# SLIDE 5: PRODUCT SHOWCASE

## The Kezi App Experience

Our "Gentle Glassmorphism" design philosophy creates an interface that feels emotionally safe, calming, and private—essential qualities for a women's health app.

### Design Philosophy
- **Deep Lavender Theme:** Calming purple tones that feel premium yet approachable
- **Glassmorphism Effects:** Modern frosted glass UI elements for visual appeal
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Simple bottom tabs for Cycle, Journal, Shop, Chat, and Settings
- **Responsive Design:** Works beautifully on all phone sizes and tablets

### Screen 1: Cycle Tracking View
The heart of Kezi. A beautiful circular cycle wheel shows your current day, phase, and fertility window at a glance. Color-coded phases (Pink for Menstrual, Teal for Follicular, Purple for Ovulation, Pink-Purple blend for Luteal) help you instantly understand where you are in your cycle. Tap for detailed predictions and historical data.

### Screen 2: Marketplace
Browse and purchase health products organized by category—Menstrual Care, Wellness, Prenatal, Fertility, and more. Each product shows price in RWF, stock availability, and merchant information. Cycle-synced recommendations highlight products most relevant to your current phase. Complete checkout with Cash on Delivery or Mobile Money.

### Screen 3: AI Chat Assistant
Your personal health companion available 24/7. Ask questions in English, French, or Kinyarwanda. The AI provides cycle-aware responses—advice that changes based on your current phase. Example: "What foods should I eat?" gets different answers during menstruation versus ovulation. Chat history is saved for reference.

### Screen 4: Wellness Journal
Log how you're feeling each day. Track mood (Happy, Calm, Anxious, Sad), symptoms (Cramps, Headache, Bloating), energy levels, and add personal notes. Over time, patterns emerge that help you understand your body's rhythms and predict how you'll feel in future cycles.

### Screen 5: Settings & Privacy
Complete control over your data and experience. Toggle Discreet Mode, set up biometric authentication, configure language preferences, manage notification settings, and export your data anytime. Privacy controls you can trust.
