The Problem

Access to reproductive health resources remains a critical challenge in Rwanda.

Limited Contraceptive Access
Only 48% of Rwandan women have access to modern contraceptives. Rural areas face even greater barriers with limited health facilities and supply chain issues.
Fragmented Health Information
Women lack centralized, culturally-sensitive platforms for cycle tracking, fertility awareness, and maternal health education in local languages.
Stigma & Privacy Concerns
Cultural barriers prevent open discussions about reproductive health. Women need discreet, private digital tools they can trust.
