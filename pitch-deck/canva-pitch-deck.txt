SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM
================================================================================

TITLE:
The Critical Gap in Women's Health

POINT 1 - Limited Contraceptive Access:
Only 48% of Rwandan women have access to modern contraceptives. Rural areas face even greater barriers with limited health facilities and supply chain challenges.

POINT 2 - Fragmented Health Information:
Women lack centralized, culturally-sensitive platforms for cycle tracking and maternal health education. Existing apps don't support Kinyarwanda or local payment methods.

POINT 3 - Stigma & Privacy Concerns:
Cultural barriers prevent open discussions about reproductive health. Women need discreet, private digital tools they can trust.

KEY STAT:
52% of Rwandan women have unmet contraceptive needs
