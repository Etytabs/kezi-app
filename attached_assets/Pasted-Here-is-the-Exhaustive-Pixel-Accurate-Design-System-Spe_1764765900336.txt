Here is the Exhaustive, Pixel-Accurate Design System Specification for the Maternal & ANC (Antenatal Care) experience in Jasmin. This module is distinct from the general shop due to its clinical yet warm aesthetic (Teal/Emerald).
I. The Core Identity (Maternal Theme)
Unlike the Cycle (Pink/Purple) or Shop (Neutral/Glass), the Maternal section uses a Teal-Primary palette to signify safety, medicine, and growth.
Semantic Role	Color Name	Hex Code	Tailwind Ref	Meaning
Primary Brand	Teal	#0D9488	teal-600	Medical Trust, Safety.
Secondary Accent	Emerald	#10B981	emerald-500	Growth, Vitality.
Action Surface	Soft Teal	#F0FDFA	teal-50	Backgrounds for cards.
Upload Widget	Dash Border	#D1D5DB	gray-300	Interactive Zones.
